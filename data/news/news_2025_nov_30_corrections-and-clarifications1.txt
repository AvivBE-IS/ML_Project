The intended reference was to the House of Lords select committee on the MPC. • We said the defence technology firm Helsing was building a factory in Portsmouth; the facility is in Plymouth and has already opened (Navies and drone makers gear up for new era of undersea warfare, 28 November, p41). • Other recently amended articles include: Why women kill ‘I almost always play it in hiding, alone’: can anyone get into free jazz, history’s most maligned music?
